Uzen

Uzen is a fertile, agricultural province, and is known for its cherries and the thistly safflower, used for dyeing. Its mountain ranges make this an easily defensible region. Three of Japan's most holy mountains stand in the province, and the area is an important site for practitioners of Shugendo, an ascetic Shintoist belief.
