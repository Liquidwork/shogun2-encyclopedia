  • Arts:

  • Units:

Effects

  • +1 to modernisation (clan development)

Description

Weapon and soldier,

Together as one spirit:

Harmony achieved.

The power of the rifle on the battlefield is in striking men down at a distance. The power of the horseman is to move like the wind. Together, they give the aggressive general a chance to make life a living hell of bullets fired from almost anywhere. There is no rest for an enemy harassed by such a force.
