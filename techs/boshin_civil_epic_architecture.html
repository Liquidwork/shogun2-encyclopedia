Epic Architecture

Enables

  • Arts:

Effects

  • -10% to the cost of constructing settlements across all provinces
  • +1 to clan-wide happiness

Description

The dream of all men.

A reality for few:

To be remembered.

The architecture of great castles, temples and palaces is intended to overawe and dwarf the ambitions of lesser men. Yet it is only by creating such magnificent structures that the rules of engineering are made plain, so that even greater buildings can be made by simple craftsmen. Who, then, is memorialised: the builders, or the masters?

Consular Court
