Mastery of Defence

Requires

  • Arts:

Effects

  • +1 defence for all units

Description

Be as the mountain:

Immovable, unchanging.

That is true defence.

In defence be without form. Without form, the attacker cannot judge your intentions, and you are not set on any one path. All ways are open to you, and this is mastery.
