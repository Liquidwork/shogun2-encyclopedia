The little stream

Winds its way down the mountain.

Cracks begin to show.

There is courage in ordering "full speed ahead" and holding to a course against the enemy, come what may. It is not a thing lightly done, but it is honest and worthy of a warrior. There is no ambiguity in the act, whatever the result. All have seen a duty done, in the old way, in a regrettably modern setting.
