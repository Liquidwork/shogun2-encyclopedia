Effects

  • +1 to modernisation (clan development)

Description

Death, unlooked for,

Reaches across the open field.

Still eyes stare at clouds.

The swordsman learns his art over many years: even the simplest of cuts. Death has meaning when it must be thought upon, the outcome is uncertain, and the enemy has a face, a name and a family. The rifle strikes from afar, after only a day's lessons. The target's courage and honour do not matter to the rifleman. He sees only an enemy uniform, impudently in the wrong place.
