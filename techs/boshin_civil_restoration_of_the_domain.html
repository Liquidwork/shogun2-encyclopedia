Spring follows winter:

Blossoms replace the cold snow.

A new house after storms.

After a storm, the farmer takes stock of his farm. What needs repairing is worked upon: the roof is mended, the ditches are cleared, and all is put back into order. Then, once more, the farmer returns to his proper labours, and everything grows thanks to his skilled attention. So it is with a nation and its people. The rulers must repair what is broken.
