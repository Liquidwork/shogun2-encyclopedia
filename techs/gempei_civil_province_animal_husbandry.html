Description

A tree in blossom.

Not every bloom is beautiful;

Some are small, less bright.

It is wise to prune these back:

Let the strong flourish anew.

A proud warlord takes pleasure in the beauty of his horses, the swiftness of his hawks and the fierceness of his hunting dogs. All reflect well on his stewardship.
