Description

Look to the horizon,

A path that all would travel:

A journey westward.

Sometimes the tide cannot be resisted, and a ship is swept away. The prudent navigator, though, has charts and, moreover, has learned the waters he sails. His vessel is not lost, washed ashore, or stranded on hidden sands. The wind blows, but he steers a safe course even in strange seas, because he learned to read the waves. His ship comes safe to port and all aboard are grateful.
