Description

Do not look behind:

Like Winter dusk, the past fades.

Now bayonets gleam.

The bow must be put aside, and the spear broken. A new army uses new weapons, and must work together in new ways to achieve victory. Officers still carry swords, but now they must carry booklearning as well as honour in their hearts. Only duty and courage remain: the unchanging watchwords of soldiers from the dawn of the world. As night falls, what else remains?
