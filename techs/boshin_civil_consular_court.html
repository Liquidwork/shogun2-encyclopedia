Description

A long way from home

The weary traveller waits,

Concealing his plans.

Unless law applies to all, law applies to no one. The outsider who thinks himself a member of a people too mighty to be judged by any except his own is a danger to all, an outlaw indeed. All must be brought within the law; all must obey the same rules. Then there is true justice for all, foreigner and Japanese alike.
