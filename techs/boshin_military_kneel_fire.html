Kneel Fire

Enables

  • Arts:

Effects

  • +1 to modernisation (clan development)

Description

Together they fire,

Bound by sacred brotherhood.

Divided they fall.

With a new gun in every hand, a modern regiment looks fierce and proud. Yet if the men cannot fire in a disciplined way, what use are the modern guns? When all fire together, few can stand before them. And so modernity must have purpose, or it is a meaningless affectation. The dictates of fashion will kill the fools who follow them.
