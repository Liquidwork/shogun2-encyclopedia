Description

Even dying can follow modern fashion.

Gatling gun towers are an improvement to the basic defences of a castle. These mechanical marvels of death from America can cut down any attackers within their range. While machinery is incredible, it surely reflects poorly on a warriors' honour to resort to such methods of making war.
