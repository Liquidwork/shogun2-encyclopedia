Character Details Panel

Character Details Panel

A character's details panel shows you information about a member of your clan. Their name, age, rank, personality traits and skills are all listed, along with details of any retainers and a summary of how all those items affect their specific abilities. You can also access the skill tree via the dedicated button on the upper right-hand side of the panel.
