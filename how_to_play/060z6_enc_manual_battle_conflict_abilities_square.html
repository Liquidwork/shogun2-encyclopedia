Yari Square

Yari Square

The yari square is an ability used by yari heroes to deflect attacks by cavalry units. Once activated, the unit forms a tight, immobile square formation with their spears presented - like a human porcupine. Although the morale of such a tightly-packed unit is very high, they are extremely vulnerable to missile attacks.
