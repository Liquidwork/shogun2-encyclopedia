Clan Effects

Clan Effects

The bonus effects awarded through dilemma decisions, missions rewards and clan traits are displayed next to the radar map. These may take the form of bonuses to income, construction and recruitment costs, the daimyo's honour or boosts to unit experience. Some are permanent - as in the case of clan traits - others are temporary, lasting just a few turns before disappearing.
