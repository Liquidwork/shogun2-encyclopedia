Faster Rowing

Faster Rowing

When faster rowing is used, the crews of cannon bune are able to row faster in order to facilitate the hit and run tactics they employ. A dedicated button will appear when faster rowing is available. Once activated, there is only a short time to use it before the ability burns out. It is then unavailable for a fixed period, thereby limiting its exploitability. When faster rowing is activated the crew will row faster than battle speed.
