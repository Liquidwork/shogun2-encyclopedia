Diplomacy

Diplomacy is the art of negotiation between clans. Use this panel to construct a deal with another clan for increased trade, formal declarations of war and peace treaties , military alliances and access through territory, marriage between your offspring, and hostages or payments to secure an agreement, amongst other things.
