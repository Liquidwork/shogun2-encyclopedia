Victory Conditions

Victory Conditions

Each clan has a set of predetermined territorial conquests that it must complete in order to attain overall victory during a campaign. These are displayed on the objectives list underneath the radar map and also on the summary tab on the clan management panel.

Influence

In Rise of the Samurai influence is a measure of the relative popularity of the major protagonists amongst the provinces. There are three categories of influence, one for each pair of clan families. A family can gain influence by sending junsatsushi into provinces to champion their clan's cause, constructing buildings that increase influence, and through events and missions.
