Bamboo Wall

Bamboo Wall

A bamboo wall can be erected during the deployment phase, providing vital protection for gunners against cavalry charges. Although they cannot be traversed by units, bamboo walls allow gunners to fire through, by the same token failing to stop any matchlock balls fired at them. It is not possible to string sections of bamboo wall together.
