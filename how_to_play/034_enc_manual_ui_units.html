Unit Details Panel

Unit Details Panel

A unit's details panel gives you important information about a particular unit type: number of men, recruitment and upkeep costs, experience, statistics, special abilities, and a short description. Clicking on the question mark button in the top right-hand corner of the panel takes you to the related unit page of this encyclopaedia.
