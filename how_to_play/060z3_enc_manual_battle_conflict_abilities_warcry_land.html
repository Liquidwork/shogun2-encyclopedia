War Cry

War Cry (Land)

The war cry is used by all warrior monk units and heroes to strike fear into the hearts of their enemies. When activated, the war cry greatly lowers the morale, movement speed and defensive capabilities of nearby enemy units - even kisho ninja, who may be hidden due to their use of the stealth ability. The war cry ability works for a short time only before burning out, at which point it is unavailable for a fixed period to limit its exploitability.

War Cry (Sea)

The crews of medium and heavy ships can utter a war cry to strike fear into the hearts of their enemies. When activated, the war cry lowers morale and reduces both the attack and the defence ability of the ship they are about to board. The war cry ability works for a short time only before burning out, at which point it is unavailable for a fixed period to limit its exploitability.
