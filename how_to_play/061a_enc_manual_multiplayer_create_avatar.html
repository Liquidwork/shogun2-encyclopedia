Creating Your Avatar

Creating Your Avatar

The avatar screen enables you to create your avatar from scratch. It is the first screen you will see upon entering multiplayer mode. Your first task is to choose your avatar's name and his clan banner through the clan setup panel. His face and equipment will be set to defaults, but can be customised to your specifications through the customise avatar panel. It is possible to create and save only one avatar. When you have completed yours, click on the confirm button to save the changes.
