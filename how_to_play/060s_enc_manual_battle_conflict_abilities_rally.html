Regroup Position

Regroup Position

Generals can use their regroup position ability to place a flag on the battlefield for troops - except those who are completely shattered - to rally to following a rout. Once the ability is activated, the cursor can be placed anywhere within the radius of the selected general's influence by left-clicking on the terrain. Rallied units will then gather close to that position. Any reinforcements due to arrive will move to and assemble at the regrouping position.
