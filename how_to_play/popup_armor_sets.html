Armour

Armour

Items of armour can be collected as your avatar develops. These are grouped into sets modelled on actual historical armour used by the samurai in combat. Some sets consist of as few as two pieces, whilst other more complex sets may have up to nine pieces and take much longer to acquire. Completing a set will result in the award of a retainer. In addition, any pieces you collect can immediately be added to the equipment your avatar is wearing via the customise avatar panel.
