Stealth

Stealth

This ability is used by kisho ninja to get very close to their chosen targets without being spotted, enabling them to run whilst hidden and deliver a more violent charge and melee attack, regardless of whether they are seen. Once activated, there is only a short time available to strike before the ability burns out. It is then unavailable for a fixed period, thereby limiting its exploitability.
