Shrapnel Shot

Shrapnel Shot

Shrapnel shot is a type of shell that explodes mid-air, showering a targeted enemy unit with deadly metal fragments. It works as an ability and, once activated, is then unavailable for a fixed period, thereby limiting its exploitability. Shrapnel shot can be used by Armstrong guns and Parrott guns.
