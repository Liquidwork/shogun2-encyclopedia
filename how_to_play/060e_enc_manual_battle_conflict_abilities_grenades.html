Blinding Grenades

Blinding Grenades

Blinding grenades are used by kisho ninja to cause disorientation that affects the enemy's movement speed, missile accuracy and ability to fight in melee. Once activated, there is only a short time available to strike before the ability burns out. It is then unavailable for a fixed period, thereby limiting its exploitability.
