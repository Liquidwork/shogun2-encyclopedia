Increased Firing Speed

Increased Firing Speed

This ability is used by upgradable tower defences to increase their rate of fire through faster turning of the crank handles by gun crews. Once activated, increased firing speed works for a short time only before burning out. The guns will then not work at all as the gunners recover from fatigue, thereby limiting exploitability of the feature.
