Options

Graphics

On the graphics panel you can adjust the battle graphics quality (according to the capacity of your PC), pick from a range of screen resolutions and select advanced options such as anti-aliasing, shadows, water effects, and brightness and gamma correction.

Sound

The sound panel enables you to change the volume of speech, sound effects and music as well as some advanced options such as the number of channels and memory used.

Controls

Listed on the controls panel there are a wealth of keyboard shortcuts that can be used instead of mouse actions. There are also options to adjust the in-game camera settings.

User Interface

The user interface panel enables you to adjust various aspects of the battle interface and whether unit/ship flags, banners and markers appear when in battle.

Credits

Select this option to view the game's credits.
