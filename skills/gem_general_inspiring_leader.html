Inspiring Leader

Effects

Level 1

  • -20% resistance to invaders in provinces conquered by this man
  • +15% to the effectiveness of the Rally ability

Level 2

  • -40% resistance to invaders in provinces conquered by this man
  • +30% to the effectiveness of the Rally ability
  • +1 to command when defending (all situations)

Description

The right word will send a man willingly to his death.

Glorious Strategist Legendary Warrior
