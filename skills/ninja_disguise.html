Master of Disguise

Effects

Level 1

  • +5% to campaign movement range for the army this agent is embedded into
  • +5% to the chance that the ninja will avoid detection when unsuccessful

Level 2

  • +10% to campaign movement range for the army this agent is embedded into
  • +10% to the chance that the ninja will avoid detection when unsuccessful

Description

Lies made flesh are not falsehood.

Criminal Contacts Gunpowder Alchemist
